Medical Prejudice across Gender

In this project, I explored gender and ethnic biases in healthcare by analyzing clinical trial data from the FDA. Using statistical methods, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and machine learning, we uncovered significant disparities in diagnosis and treatment across demographics. This research highlights the urgent need for inclusive clinical trial methodologies and reforms to ensure equity in healthcare outcomes.
