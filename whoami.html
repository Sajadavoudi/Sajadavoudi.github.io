About me

Hey, I'm Sajad, currently pursuing a Master’s in Bioinformatics at Sorbonne Université, with a background in computer science. After spending a couple of years diving deep into web development, enhancing my skills with various technologies, I've now shifted my focus toward the fascinating fields of data science and machine learning.

My journey has led me to explore how these powerful tools can be applied to tackle challenges in biology and psychology. One of my recent projects involved studying medical prejudice in healthcare systems, where I applied data analysis and machine learning techniques to uncover significant insights. You can check out the poster that project here: Medical Prejudice Study.

What drives me is the constant desire to learn and evolve in a world where technology is ever-changing. I see myself as a lifelong learner, excited to dig into complex problems and find innovative solutions.
